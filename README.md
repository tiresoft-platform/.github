# 🚀 TireSoft

TireSoft is a next-generation SaaS platform built to modernize fleet tire management.

We help fleet operators gain full visibility and control over the entire tire lifecycle — from procurement to mounting, repairs, retreading, and final disposal.

## Why TireSoft?

Tires are one of the highest recurring operational costs in fleet management — yet they’re often tracked manually or across disconnected systems.

TireSoft centralizes everything into a single intelligent platform.

## ⚙️ Core Features

- Real-time tire inventory management
- Vehicle mounting & dismount tracking
- Repair and retread workflows
- Warehouse and supplier management
- Full lifecycle event history per tire
- Operational alerts & reporting

## 🧠 Built for Scale

- Kotlin + Spring Boot backend
- Angular (latest) frontend
- RESTful API-first architecture
- JWT authentication
- Multi-tenant ready
- Designed for SaaS growth

## 🎯 Our Mission

To bring operational intelligence and cost transparency to fleet tire management.
